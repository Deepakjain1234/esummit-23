What’s in the box?
Business Plan Competition
Business Plan is a contest of the best minds based on the idea of presenting a lean canvas in front of a panel of experts and pitching them the ideas.
Stock Trading Simulation
Stock Trading Simulation provides a risk-free environment with a realistic replica of the Dalal Street to try and learn relevant skills needed for trading before they trade in real life.
Crypto Trading Simulation
Crypto Trading Simulation is a cluster of a workshop on gaining a deeper understanding of digital currencies and a contest of trading with the replica of numerous cryptocurrencies.
Hackathon
Hackathon is a 48-hour long event for all the coding enthusiasts to win exciting prizes by utilizing their coding skills to develop a prototype solution for real world problems.
Creator’s Camp
Creator’s Camp is a fun event aimed at introducing social media marketing tools and strategies through a series of exercises that focus on creatively promoting companies and goods.
Start Up Expo
Startup Expo provides a platform to connect the most disruptive startups from all over the country and investors while pitching their innovative ideas and earning valuable resources like funding, co-working space etc.
Guest Lecture
Watch successful people spilling their success secrets! Guest sessions will feature renowned International speakers from different domains.
IPL Auction
IPL Auction is a replica of the bidding event, challenging judgemental skills, budget management and strategizing skills to form the best possible team.
Comedic Pitshop
Workshops are a comprehensive place with intensive exercises and numerous sessions by industrial experts on concepts that require guidance to master them.

Our Past Events
E-Summit is the annual signature event of E-Cell NIT-B, instilling entrepreneurial spirit. The goal of E-Summit’22 was to enhance the financial education of young students and encourage startups and small businesses. The event had been a great success in guiding people and training them in their respective fields. E-Cell was honored to have the presence of renowned guests like Nisant Mohta, Rachna Suneja, Aswini Bajaj, and many more.

E-Summit
E-Carnival was a two-tier event consisting of Book Fair and Meme War, instilling and improving the ability of creative thinking. The Book Fair in partnership with some online bookstores was carried out to induce a reading culture among students and encourage bookselling startups. On the other hand, Meme War aimed to promote a non-conventional method of marketing using memes.

E-Carnival

Pe-Charcha is the podcast series of E-Cell NIT-B that airs every fortnight on Youtube, ApplePodcasts, Spotify and Anchor. With intense discussion on various topics, it is the ideal podcast for the curious minds. The renowned speakers mentor aspiring enthusiasts while also sharing the experience of the speakers from a wide range of fields including Entrepreneurship, Sports, Finance, and many more.

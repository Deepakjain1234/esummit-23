About
With the aim to equip startups or individuals having a startup concept in the Early stages, with the know-how of building an ideal business plan and pitching it in front of Venture capitalists, incubators and investors, E-Cell, NITB, brings you the Business Plan Contest. The event is aimed at providing an opportunity to get hands-on application of knowledge gained from the interactive sessions.
Eligibility
An entrepreneurial mindset and a “STUDENT” ID card, is all you need to be a part of Central India’s Largest Entrepreneurs Fest. Cross-College teams are also allowed. for instance, the participant should be enrolled in -
• High school and above level of schooling
• Undergraduate Course
• Postgraduate Course
• Professional Courses
• Any other course recognised by the government of India/ respective country
• Note
The participants can register with a team of up to 4, but up to two members will be provided with offline accommodation and travel.

• Event Phases -
The contest is based on the idea of presenting a business plan in front of a panel of experts and pitching ideas to them. Apart from the cash prize of 50,000 INR, the winners will be allowed to incubate their idea at partnered incubation centres and get invested from partnered early-stage investors. The teams will also get credits from partnered services for boosting their idea to the next level.
•
There will be three phases to this competition:
1.Learning Phase (Online):
A guest/speaker will explain how to make a Lean Canvas. And also, an online session will take place on how to pitch your idea exceptionally.
2.Preliminary Phase (Online):
Teams will submit their idea by making a lean canvas.
3.Mentorship Phase (Online):
Selected 25 teams will get online mentorship from experts And get ready for offline pitching.
4.Pitching Round:
The 25 teams with the best idea will be invited to pitch their ideas to the panel of experts, investors, incubators and government officials. Each team will be given 4 minutes, followed by a 6-minute Q&A session.
